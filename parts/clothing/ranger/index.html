Description:

Don't let the Ranger Cargos' mellow, off-trail presence mislead you, as Fox Racing's mountain-bike aptitude is abundant in these ride anything, ride anywhere shorts. From lush northern forests to desert singletrack, the Ranger Cargos' two-way stretch poly shell ensures cool, dry comfort and unlimited range of motion. Out on the trail, Fox treats you to a six-panel, moisture-wicking, and breathable liner with Pro Form chamois, which provides not only support, but relief from irritation, too. The anatomic liner is detachable, allowing you to pair it with knickers and pants for cooler climates; and best of all, the Rangers turn into a pair of technical shorts for travels between trailheads.

As for storage space, the Ranger Cargos have plenty; this includes two pedal-friendly hand pockets for quick access to electronic devices, one zipped cargo for secure storage, and a flapped cargo for streamlined convenience to nutrition.
