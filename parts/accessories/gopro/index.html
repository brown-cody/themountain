GOPRO HERO 5 BLACK

Back to Accessories

Description:

Representing the next generation of the GoPro's action cameras, the GoPro HERO5 Black records your adventures in the stunning clarity of 4K video and 12 megapixel photos. A host of new features, such as voice-activated control, two-inch touchscreen display, and intuitive one-button operation, make capturing awe-inspiring footage easier than ever. Upgraded over the previous version, its rubberized design is waterproof to 10 meters without requiring a separate housing. Another design upgrade, three microphones provide stereo playback without excess wind noise ruining the audio quality.

Specficiations:

  • Waterproof to 10 meters (33 feet) without housing
  • 2-inch touchscreen + one-button control + voice activation
  • Advanced image stabilization for smoother playback
  • Mind-blowing 4K footage at 30 frames per second (fps)
  • HD footage: 1440p at 80 fps, 1080p at 120 fps
  • 12 megapixel photos in RAW and WDR formats
  • WiFi + Bluetooth connectivity with GoPro app & GoPro Plus
