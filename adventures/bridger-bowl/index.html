BRIDGER BOWL

Bridger Bowl

A comfortable set of trails for the beginning mountain biker, yet still fun for the more experienced rider

Back to Adventures

$99

Description:

Bridger Bowl was included in Mountain Bike Parks' 2014 Riders' Choice Awards for the top five mountain bike parks in the Northwest. They offer a wide variety of difficulty levels, all the way down to beginning.

When you purchase the tour, you get a shuttle ride from downtown Bozeman, guided trail tour along the top of the Bridger Mountain Range, and some of the most breathtaking views in the United States.

Details:

  • Bike Rental: Optional $50 - $150, depending on model
  • Distance: 12 miles
  • Time: 3 - 5 hours
  • Difficulty: Beginning - Intermediate
  • Price: $99
  • Availability: May - October
