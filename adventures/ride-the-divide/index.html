Ride the Divide

Join us this year for our annual "Ride the Divide" event, where you can participate in a guided mountain bike tour on the Continental Divide Trail.

Back to Adventures

$499

Description:

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route is our premier off-pavement cycling route, crisscrossing the Continental Divide north to south. This route is defined by the word "remote." Its remoteness equates with spectacular terrain and scenery. The entire route is dirt-road and mountain-pass riding every day. In total, it has over 200,000 feet of elevation gain.

We leave Polaris, Montana and take the trail 510 miles to South Pass City, Wyoming. Participants will need to pack food, a water filtration system, and a sleeping bag at minimum.

We take care of all transportation before and after the tour, and complete all of the paperwork for permits and fees.

Book as an individual or a group. Only advanced riders should participate.

Details:

  • Bike Rental: Not available
  • Distance: 510 miles
  • Time: 10 Days
  • Difficulty: Advanced Riders Only
  • Price: $499
  • Dates: June 4 - June 14, 2017
