Description:

Do you love 29-inch big-wheeled bikes but always wanted something more aggressive than the XC-oriented offerings? This is why we made the ultra-aggressive, trailblazing, industry-changing Honzo AL. A progressive geometry gives the Honzo a ride that is comparable to full-suspension bikes but has all the familiar traits of a sturdy and dependable hardtail. Time to ride!

Specficiations:

  • Frame Material: Kona 6061 Aluminum Butted
  • Wheel Size: 29
  • Wheels: WTB SX25
  • Front Suspension: 120mm
  • Fork: RockShox Recon Silver RL 120mm
  • Crankset: RaceFace Ride
  • Drivetrain: Sram NX 1x 11spd
  • Cockpit: Kona XC/BC bar and stem, Kona Race Light Log grips
  • Brakes: Shimano Hydraulic
  • Front Tire: Maxxis Ardent WB 29x2.4"
  • Rear Tire: Maxxis Ardent WB 29x2.25"
  • Saddle: Kona Trail
